# Rhema App Glossary

## Impression

### Share your impression:
When adding your perception of what you believe God is communicating during prayer.

### Record an impression:
The act of documenting a thought, feeling, or insight received during prayer.

### Impressions:
A collection of insights or perceived divine communications shared by users in response to a prayer.

## Rhema

### Definition:
A Greek word meaning "an utterance" or "thing said," often interpreted in Christian contexts as a specific word from God or a divinely inspired message.

### Rhema App:
The name of this application, reflecting its purpose of facilitating the sharing of divine insights and communications.

## Prayer

### Create a prayer:
Initiating a focused topic or intention for users to collectively seek divine guidance about.

### Open prayer:
A prayer that is still active and accepting new impressions from users.

### Closed prayer:
A prayer that has concluded its active period and no longer accepts new impressions.

## Group

### Prayer group:
A collection of users who come together to focus on specific prayer topics or intentions.
